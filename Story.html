To this day my friends will still poke fun at my speech, and they do not know how badly it hurts.

I cannot remember a time when I was not bullied. I believe it started from my school life, kids would pull me across the playground by my bag and chase me around before school started and tried to bully me because i was not good in my studies and embarrass me. It got worse as the years went on. I remember 3rd grade. A group of kids who thought I was too quiet took out my class test papers at basketball court and said that I am abnormal, I am mental retarded and i can't study well. Even my teachers thought the same since nobody liked me in school I had none to stand for me. They choked me and punched my stomach. Since i was slow in everything my school said that they can't keep me anymore and that i should be in special school rather than the normal one. I changed my school but i didn't knew that i will be bullied even here. I was depressed and scared all the time. High school is where things hit the hardest. It was freshman year, I was a bigger girl ( very overweight ). I was bullied about my weight and my looks. I think it wasn't the bullying that made me feel so bad it was what was going on inside my head that made things hard. I could not function at all, I was so out of it. I needed help. I began to do self harm. NO boys wanted to go out me just because i was FAT since all my friends- i mean my classmates had boyfriend and girlfriend; I was lost, And every-time i reached out for help it always backfired and i was just a bunch of drama to my family and my school. After so much of bullies I started to laugh when kids called me by names and everyone who used to bully me thought that i was going crazy and left me alone. Now here i am, I am feeling a lot better. But whatever happens just know that your problems DO NOT define who you are. You are more than your depression and pain. You are strong. Now i am dating a hot guy who thinks that me being chubby with my natural beauty is the most beautiful thing in the world. Just wanna say that be you; you don't have to change for nobody. Love yourself and that what's will make ever pain go away. ----NIVA
